## LG S24AHN

### Signals:
Pulse length coding (determined by the width of the OFF pulse)

* SEPARATE (ON): 8428µs
* INTRODUCE: 4210µs
* HIGH: 1590µs
* LOW: 520µs


### Command format:
```text
|  8 bits  |  8 bits   |      8 bits       |   4 bits   |
|=======================================================|
| 10001000 | [COMMAND] | [COMMAND PAYLOAD] | [CHECKSUM] |
```

#### Command types
```text
=====================================
|| 00000000 | On                   ||
||---------------------------------||
|| 00001000 | Cool                 ||
||---------------------------------||
|| 00001001 | Dehumidify           ||
||---------------------------------||
|| 00001011 | Auto                 ||
||---------------------------------||
|| 00010011 | Swing                ||
||---------------------------------||
|| 101000XX | Sleep (10bit payload)||
||---------------------------------||
|| 10110000 | Cancel               ||
||---------------------------------||
|| 11000000 | Off                  ||
=====================================
```

#### Payload for On/Cool/Dehumidify/Auto
```text
=============================
|    4 bits     |  4 bits   |
|===========================|
| [TEMPERATURE] |  [SPEED]  |
=============================
```

#### Payload for Sleep
```text
H = Hours sleep - 1
==================================
|  4 bits  |  4 bits  |  2 bits  |   
|================================|
|    [H]   | [NOT H]  |    00    |
==================================
```
